Relax! Help is coming your way!

We strongly recommend that every Faraday user starts by reading our official documentation.

If after reading it you are still in the need of some assistance then we have some recommendations:

  • * Maybe someone already had this question before, have you tried the frequently asked questions part of our docs?
  • * Not a question but a problem, it could help to take a look at the troubleshooting guide
  • * We also have a bunch of interesting video demos that may help
  • * Open a ticket





Still can't fix it? You can reach us and find other Faraday users on:

  • * User forum - forum.faradaysec.com
  • * IRC chat - #faraday-dev in freenode
  • * Mailing list - groups.google.com/d/forum/faradaysec
  • * Twitter - twitter.com/faradaysec
